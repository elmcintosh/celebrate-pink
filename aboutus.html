About Celebrate Pink

Celebrate Pink is a non-profit organization based out of Cheyenne, Wyoming that is dedicated to fundraising, outreach, and providing grants to individuals undergoing treatment for breast cancer.

We were created to assist those who are undergoing breast cancer treatment.
It is widely known that breast cancer is not only a debilitating process but that it is exhorbitantly expensive for individuals as well as their families. Our goal is to provide assistance for these individuals that can help to improve their quality of life while undergoing treatment.

How We Can Help

We can assist individuals by providing monetary grants to help with personal, non-medical needs such as:

Personal Needs

Personal Desires

Personal Dreams

That are outside of medical treatments and are not covered by insurance.
