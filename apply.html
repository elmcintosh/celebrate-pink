Qualifications:

Individuals who wish to apply for a grant from Celebrate Pink should meet the following general qualifications

Individuals currently undergoing treatment for breast cancer.
Individuals who currently reside in Laramie or Albany County, Wyoming
Individuals who are currently undergoing treatment in Cheyenne, WY
**Grants are also possible for individuals experiencing recurrent breast cancer.**

If you or someone you know meets the above qualifications please print out and submit the following application and mail it to us. We look foward to hearing from you!

Send Applications to:

Celebrate Pink
Grant Committee
PO Box 2763
Cheyenne, WY 82003
